Halal Cuisine Recommendations

Eid Halal Korean Bangi

Eid Halal Korean Bangi offers authentic Korean cuisine with a halal twist. Enjoy a wide range of delicious Korean dishes prepared with halal ingredients, including bulgogi, bibimbap, and Korean barbecue. With a cozy atmosphere and friendly staff, Eid Halal Korean Bangi provides an unforgettable dining experience. Whether you're a fan of spicy kimchi or savory dumplings, Eid Halal Korean Bangi has something for everyone.

Yosma, London

Yosma offers authentic Turkish cuisine with a modern twist, all prepared using halal ingredients. Located in London, Yosma provides a vibrant dining experience with its lively atmosphere and contemporary decor. Indulge in traditional Turkish dishes such as kebabs, mezes, and baklava, all crafted with care and attention to detail. Don't forget to try their signature cocktails and desserts for a complete dining experience that will transport you to the streets of Istanbul.

The Meat Co.

The Meat Co. is a premium halal steakhouse offering a diverse menu of high-quality meats and gourmet dishes. With locations around the world, including London, The Meat Co. is renowned for its succulent steaks, grilled meats, and indulgent desserts. Experience world-class dining and impeccable service at The Meat Co. From tender ribeye steaks to mouthwatering lamb chops, every dish at The Meat Co. is prepared to perfection, ensuring a memorable dining experience for all guests.
