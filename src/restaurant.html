Restaurant Recommendations

Odette Restaurant, Singapore

Award-winning modern French cuisine located in the National Gallery Singapore. With a focus on seasonality, the menu at Odette offers innovative dishes crafted from the finest ingredients sourced from around the world. Enjoy a culinary journey through meticulously prepared courses in an elegant and sophisticated setting.

Central, Lima

Renowned Peruvian cuisine with a focus on sustainable and organic ingredients. Central, led by chef Virgilio Martínez, offers a unique dining experience inspired by the diverse ecosystems of Peru. Each dish reflects the altitude and biodiversity of different regions, showcasing the richness of Peruvian gastronomy in a contemporary and creative way.

Disfrutar, Barcelona

Creative Mediterranean cuisine with innovative and playful presentations. At Disfrutar, chefs Mateu Casañas, Oriol Castro, and Eduard Xatruch combine traditional Mediterranean flavors with modern cooking techniques to create a dining experience that surprises and delights. Expect an array of visually stunning dishes that push the boundaries of culinary artistry.
